Touch events

Touch callback are triggered on touch device (tablet, mobile phone).
Active class is applied on touch and click.
Open console for more detail.

Example:

Click or touch me! I will always stay active

Click or touch me! I will active for 2 seconds

Click or touch me, I will stay active until google send me a response.

Status info:

Touch state: {{state}}

Response received: {{responseReceived}}

Directive

  • ng-touch-start: trigger callback on touchstart event from browser
  • ng-touch-finish: trigger callback on touchend touchcancel event from browser
  • ng-active-on-click: how many time the "active" class will be visible (in ms). if "pending", it will wait until network has completed request.
